Does ChatGPT enhance student learning?

A course project on AI and education

Overview Results Study Example AI Transparency

Overview

This website presents a structured overview of current research on the impact of ChatGPT on student learning. It is based on a recent meta-analysis and includes one selected individual study example.

Main Results

Research suggests that ChatGPT can support learning in several ways.

  • improvement in academic performance
  • positive effects on motivation
  • support for higher-order thinking
  • reduction of mental effort

At the same time, not all studies show positive results, and the impact depends strongly on how ChatGPT is used.

Individual Study Example

One selected study examined whether ChatGPT-3.5 improves students’ essay writing. The results showed no clear improvement in essay quality or writing speed, which makes this study especially interesting.

It highlights that the use of AI does not automatically lead to better learning outcomes.
