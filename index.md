# Does ChatGPT enhance student learning?
![ChatGPT and learning](images/chatgpt-learning-cover.png)

## Overview

This website presents a structured overview of current research on the impact of ChatGPT and generative artificial intelligence on student learning. The content is based on a recent meta-analysis and is part of a university project in the field of digital psychology.

The goal of this project is to critically evaluate whether and how ChatGPT can support learning processes in educational contexts.

---

## Why this topic?

Artificial intelligence tools such as ChatGPT are rapidly transforming education. Students increasingly use AI for:

- writing essays  
- solving problems  
- generating explanations  
- studying and preparing for exams  

However, an important question remains:

> Does ChatGPT actually improve learning or does it just make tasks easier?

This website explores this question based on scientific evidence.

## Key insights (short preview)

Research suggests that ChatGPT can:

- improve academic performance  
- support higher-order thinking  
- enhance motivation  

However:

- effects depend strongly on how the tool is used  
- not all studies show positive results  
- overreliance and lack of critical thinking remain concerns
